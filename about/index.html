About Xinyang Li Research Platform

Health-data systems methodology research — healthcare interoperability, clinical intelligence, and secure health information exchange.

Xinyang Li is a health-data systems methodology researcher specializing in healthcare interoperability, clinical intelligence, and secure health information exchange.

His research focuses on developing standards-based approaches for transforming heterogeneous healthcare data into interoperable, explainable, and governance-aware digital health systems.

His work includes three interconnected research areas:

Research Areas

Healthcare Interoperability

Development of FHIR R4-based interoperability frameworks, including canonical data modeling, terminology reconciliation, structural transformation, and data-quality governance.

Clinical Intelligence

Development of explainable clinical decision-support methodologies integrating remote patient monitoring, machine learning, and interoperable clinical artifacts.

Secure Health Data Governance

Development of security-aware healthcare data exchange frameworks incorporating identity governance, consent management, auditability, and privacy-preserving data access.
